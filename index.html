Black Panther is a Marvel superhero known for his superhuman abilities, intelligence, and powerful vibranium suit.

Spider-Man: Peter Parker is a high school student who gains spider-like abilities, using them to fight crime as Spider-Man.

Captain America: Steve Rogers is a super-soldier who fights for American ideals with his shield and leadership skills.

Iron Man: Tony Stark is a genius inventor who builds a high-tech suit to fight evil and protect the world.

Hulk: Bruce Banner is a brilliant scientist who transforms into a green, rage-filled monster with immense strength.

Thor: As the Norse god of thunder, Thor wields a powerful hammer and battles villains with his godly strength.

Deadpool: A mercenary with a twisted sense of humor, Deadpool has a healing factor and advanced fighting skills.
